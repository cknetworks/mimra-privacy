Privacy Policy — Mimra

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CK Networks ("we", "us") handles information in the Mimra mobile app ("the App"). If you have questions, contact us at cknetworks@gmail.com.

Summary

Mimra is a language-learning flashcard app. We collect the minimum needed to run your account, sync your study data across your devices, and generate translations, audio, definitions, and images for the cards you create. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads, and we do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information we collect

Account information. If you create an account, we store your email address and an encrypted password (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase) so you can sign in and sync across devices.

Content you create. The phrases, words, lists, images you choose, generated audio, and your study progress and statistics. This is stored on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to our backend (Supabase) so it is available on your other devices.

Voice recordings (pronunciation practice). When you use pronunciation practice, the App records short audio clips using your microphone and sends them to our speech-to-text provider (OpenAI Whisper) to score your pronunciation. The clip is used only to produce that transcription and is not stored on our servers afterward.

Device and diagnostic information. Basic technical data needed to operate the app (for example, network status). The App does not include third-party analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking software, and does not build advertising profiles about you.

How your card content is generated (third-party processing)

To create cards, text you enter and content you request are sent — through our secure server proxy — to the following providers, solely to return the requested result:

  • Google Cloud Translation — translating your text.
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech — generating audio for your cards.
  • OpenAI — refining translations and generating word definitions/senses and list suggestions.
  • OpenAI Whisper — transcribing your pronunciation recordings.
  • Pixabay — searching for images you can attach to cards.

These providers process the submitted content to fulfil the request and under their own terms. We do not send them your account credentials.

How we use information

  • To provide and operate the App (accounts, sign-in, cross-device sync).
  • To generate the translations, audio, definitions, and images you request.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.

We do not use your information for advertising and do not sell or rent it.

Data sharing

We share information only with the service providers listed above (our backend and the content-generation providers) strictly to operate the features you use, and where required by law. We do not share your data for their independent marketing.

Data retention and deletion

Your synced data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account from within the App (Settings), which removes your account and associated synced data from our backend. Locally stored data is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data. To request deletion by email, contact cknetworks@gmail.com.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted transport, provider-managed authentication) to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

International users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our providers operate (e.g. the United States).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

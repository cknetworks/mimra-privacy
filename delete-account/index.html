Delete Your Account — Mimra

CK Networks · Mimra

This page explains how to delete your Mimra account and the data associated with it. If you have any questions, contact us at cknetworks@gmail.com.

Delete from within the app

If you still have the app installed and can sign in:

  1. Open Mimra and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your account and its synced data from our backend.

Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app (for example, you have uninstalled it), email cknetworks@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and ask us to delete your account. We will verify your request and process it within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account (email address and authentication record).
  • Your synced content — phrases, words, lists, generated audio references, and your study progress and statistics stored on our backend.

Voice clips used for pronunciation practice are not retained on our servers, so there is nothing to delete there. Any data stored only on your device is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Data retention

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. We retain no personal account data after a deletion is completed, except where a limited retention period is required by law.
